Ship Movement limited by movement speed and agility
You can move at any time during your turn (before, after, or during actions).
Ship Actions 1/turn
You can perform these only if you're in the Spelljammer position, and only one person can hold that position at a time.
Officer Actions 1/officer/turn
You can take one of these actions from the available list for your position.
Officer Positions
Any number of characters can concurrently serve in the same officer position (except Spelljammer), in which case they share the responsibilities and duties of that position.
Ship Critical Effects
Critical Effects occur to the ship on a critical fail, and alter its capabilities in a variety of ways. They remain in effect until repaired by the "Repair Critical Effect" Officer Action.
