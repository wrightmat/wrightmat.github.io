#npc [[Arcanix]]

Gender:: Male
Occupation:: Lord Mayor
Race:: Human

Raulo ir'Trannick is a human wizard and member of Arcanix's Arcane Congress, with a special role as the administrator of the school's application exams. He is also the patriarch of the noble house of Trannick and serves as lord mayor of the city of Arcanix. As his family runs a hippogriff transport business, he is known for using a hippogriff to travel between the floating towers and the ground. His family is a well-established landholding family in the region around Lake Galifar. These various titles and holdings provide ir'Trannick and his house with a great deal of prestige, and he tends to be the person who greets new wizards visiting Arcanix.
